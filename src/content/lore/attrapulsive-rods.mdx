title: Attrapulsive Rods
tags:

---
A chunk of some attrapulsive material crafted to induce a specific effect,
often as part of a [stable machine](lore:stable-machines). Rods designed to be
held by a person in order to create effects via mental coupling are called
[handles](lore:coupling-handles).
