'Firthan' is the [absolute direction](lore:directions) along the bridge going
left if you are facing [morningside](lore:morningside), the edge of the bridge
where the sun rises.

At the far firthan end of the bridge, you can find [the Bridgehand](lore:the-bridgehand).
