Citizens of Longbridge use an [absolute system of directions](lore:directions).
'Firthan' is the direction along the bridge going left if you are
facing [morningside](lore:morningside), the edge of the bridge where
the sun rises.

At the far firthan end of the bridge, you can find [the Bridgehand](lore:the-bridgehand).
