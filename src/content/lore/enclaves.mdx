tags:

The city of Longbridge is divided into neighbourhoods called enclaves, each
with its own distinct subculture and history. Each enclave is centered on top
of one of the bridge's major pillars, where there is a wider section normally
known as a 'square', forming a central plaza from which the enclave grows.
