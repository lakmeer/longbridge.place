Velvetblossom is a common plant that grows in the cracks on the
[eveningside](lore:eveningside) of the bridge near the [tern](lore:tern) end.
It is small and produces clusters of tiny blue flowers. It's name comes from
the fine silvery fuzz that covers it's leaves on both sides.

Extract of velvetblossom is used in the [alchemical recipe](lore:mixing) for
the Lowsleep [elixir](lore:elixir).
