The residents of the city of Longrbridge know each other as 'citizens',
except in [the Cult of the Hand](lore:cult-of-the-hand) where they are known as
'members'.

It is customary to address strangers with the [auxiliary title name](lore:auxiliary-names)
'Citizen' when nothing more specific is known.
