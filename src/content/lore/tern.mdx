'Tern' is the [absolute direction](lore:directions) along the bridge going
right if you are facing [morningside](lore:morningside), the edge of the bridge
where the sun rises.

At the far tern end of the bridge, the bridge [is destroyed](lore:the-broken-end),
and abruptly ends.
