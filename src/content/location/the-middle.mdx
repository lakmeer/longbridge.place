Built on the giant central pillar, The Middle widens out significantly compared
to the rest of the Bridge. Home of the spectacular
[Keystone Fortress](lore:keystone-fortress), which in turn houses 
the [University](location:the-university-in-the-middle),
the [Observatory](lore:the-observatory), and
the [Administration Chambers](lore:administration-chambers).
Keystone is surrounded by a huge and lush [tiered garden](location:presidium)
with many public spaces. It is a popular destination for visitors from all over
the bridge, whether on business or leisure.
