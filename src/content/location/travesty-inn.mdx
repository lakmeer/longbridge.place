The Travesty Inn is a popular dive bar and commonhouse in
[Makren](location:makren). It has been run for over 20 years by Travesty Tym,
formally a well-known underworld fixer with a 'loveable rogue' reputation, now
retired from rogueish hijinks, allegedly.
