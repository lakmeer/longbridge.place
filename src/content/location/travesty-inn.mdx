The Travesty Inn is a popular dive bar and commonhouse in
[Mackintosh](location:mackintosh). It has been run for over 20 years by
Travesty Tym, formally a well-known underworld fixer with a 'loveable rogue'
reputation, now retired from rogueish hijinks, allegedly.
