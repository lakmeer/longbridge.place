Loom is a district dedicated to textile production and trade, located 5
enclaves firthan of The Middle. It is home to weavers, tailors, and dyers who
create intricate fabrics, clothing, and tapestries for the city.

The streets are adorned with hanging textiles, showcasing the craftsmanship and
artistry of it is residents. It is on the firthan end due to proximity with the 
[Bridgehand](lore:the-bridgehand), where most of the raw materials come from.
